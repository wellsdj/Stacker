How to play

Tap or press Space to drop the moving block onto the tower.

Line it up dead-center for a perfect drop — perfects keep the full width, and a streak of 5 in a row grows your tower 5×.

Pass the Statue of Liberty, Space Needle, Eiffel Tower, Empire State Building, One World Trade Center, Shanghai Tower and Burj Khalifa — then keep climbing all the way to the atmosphere at 100,000 m.

Every landmark you pass is celebrated again on every new run, so you always know what you have built beyond and what comes next.

Every 100 m still unlocks a new tower skin, and every metre from a 5× perfect counts toward the Burj.
